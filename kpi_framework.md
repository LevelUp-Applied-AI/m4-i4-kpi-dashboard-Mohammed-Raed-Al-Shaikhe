# KPI Framework — Amman Digital Market

---

## KPI 1

- **Name:** Total Revenue
- **Definition:** Total monetary value generated from all completed orders in the dataset.
- **Formula:**  
  \[
  \text{Total Revenue} = \sum (\text{quantity} \times \text{unit_price})
  \]
- **Data Source (tables/columns):**
  - `order_items.quantity`
  - `products.unit_price`
  - `orders.order_id`
- **Baseline Value:** 48,701.5
- **Interpretation:**  
  This KPI represents the overall size of the business and is the main indicator of financial performance.

---

## KPI 2 (Time-Based)

- **Name:** Monthly Revenue Trend
- **Definition:** Total revenue generated each month to track business growth over time.
- **Formula:**  
  \[
  \text{Monthly Revenue} = \sum (\text{revenue}) \text{ grouped by month}
  \]
- **Data Source (tables/columns):**
  - `orders.order_date`
  - `order_items.quantity`
  - `products.unit_price`
- **Baseline Value:** (from `monthly_revenue` output)
- **Interpretation:**  
  Shows how revenue changes over time and helps identify growth or decline trends.

---

## KPI 3 (Time-Based)

- **Name:** Month-over-Month Revenue Growth Rate
- **Definition:** Measures the percentage change in revenue between consecutive months.
- **Formula:**  
  \[
  \text{MoM Growth} = \frac{\text{Current Month Revenue} - \text{Previous Month Revenue}}{\text{Previous Month Revenue}}
  \]
- **Data Source (tables/columns):**
  - `orders.order_date`
  - `order_items.quantity`
  - `products.unit_price`
- **Baseline Value:** (latest value from `monthly_growth`)
- **Interpretation:**  
  Indicates whether the business is growing or declining on a monthly basis.

---

## KPI 4 (Cohort-Based)

- **Name:** Revenue by Customer Cohort
- **Definition:** Revenue generated by customers grouped by their first purchase month.
- **Formula:**  
  \[
  \text{Cohort Revenue} = \sum (\text{revenue}) \text{ grouped by first purchase month}
  \]
- **Data Source (tables/columns):**
  - `customers.customer_id`
  - `orders.order_date`
  - `order_items.quantity`
  - `products.unit_price`
- **Baseline Value:** (from `cohort_revenue`)
- **Interpretation:**  
  Helps analyze customer retention and lifetime value across different acquisition groups.

---

## KPI 5

- **Name:** Average Order Value (AOV)
- **Definition:** The average amount spent per order.
- **Formula:**  
  \[
  \text{AOV} = \frac{\sum (\text{revenue})}{\text{number of orders}}
  \]
- **Data Source (tables/columns):**
  - `order_items.order_id`
  - `order_items.quantity`
  - `products.unit_price`
- **Baseline Value:** 109.94
- **Interpretation:**  
  Measures how much customers spend per transaction and helps evaluate pricing and upselling strategies.
